Magical Design

Our clients tell us we're magical because we design dresses that suit each client perfectly and ensure you look the best you could; it is your forever day! Our fitters ensure the dress is hugging the places it should, and staying away from the places it shouldn't - right up until the day of your wedding.

In home consults

We know that getting to our boutique isn't always possible when you're planning other aspects of your wedding. We offer in-home fittings to New York residents during business hours.

Female Design

As a wedding boutique, we specialise in outfitting the women of the wedding. Although we do not offer design for pieces other than dresses, we have close relationships with other designers who do. Just let us know you need some recommendations, and we'll be happy to help.
